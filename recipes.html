Recipes

Butter Chicken

Ingredients:

  • Chicken
  • Butter
  • Tomatoes
  • Cream
  • Spices

Instructions:

  1. Marinate the chicken with spices.
  2. Cook the chicken in butter until golden brown.
  3. Add tomatoes and cook until soft.
  4. Blend the tomatoes to make a smooth sauce.
  5. Add cream and cooked chicken to the sauce.
  6. Simmer until the chicken is fully cooked.

Chicken Tikka Masala

Ingredients:

  • Chicken
  • Yogurt
  • Tomatoes
  • Onions
  • Spices

Instructions:

  1. Marinate the chicken in yogurt and spices.
  2. Grill the chicken until cooked.
  3. Prepare a sauce with tomatoes, onions, and spices.
  4. Add the grilled chicken to the sauce and simmer.

Paneer Tikka

Ingredients:

  • Paneer (Indian cottage cheese)
  • Yogurt
  • Spices (like tandoori masala)
  • Lemon juice

Instructions:

  1. Marinate the paneer in yogurt, spices, and lemon juice.
  2. Grill or bake until slightly charred and cooked through.

Samosa

Ingredients:

  • Potatoes
  • Peas
  • Spices
  • Pastry dough

Instructions:

  1. Prepare a spiced potato and pea filling.
  2. Roll out the pastry dough and cut into triangles.
  3. Place the filling in the center and fold the pastry.
  4. Fry or bake until golden brown.

Chicken Tandoori

Ingredients:

  • Chicken
  • Yogurt
  • Tandoori spices
  • Lemon juice

Instructions:

  1. Marinate the chicken in yogurt, tandoori spices, and lemon juice.
  2. Grill or bake until fully cooked and slightly charred.

Garlic Chicken

Ingredients:

  • Chicken
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Soy sauce
  • Spices

Instructions:

  1. Marinate the chicken in garlic, ginger, and soy sauce.
  2. Stir-fry the chicken with additional spices until cooked through.
